About the Summit

The Ijebu Muslim Business Summit (IMBS) 2.0 is a premier gathering designed to inspire, equip, and connect Muslim entrepreneurs, professionals, and business leaders. Rooted in Islamic ethics and forward-thinking enterprise principles, the Summit provides practical insights for building resilient and sustainable businesses.

The 2025 edition successfully brought together business leaders, scholars, youth innovators, cooperative societies, and professionals who exchanged insights on business growth, digital transformation, and ethical enterprise development. The Summit recorded commendable achievements such as increased awareness of halal finance, stronger interbusiness connections, youth interest in entrepreneurship, and the establishment of preliminary working groups for future collaborations. Feedback from participants highlighted the need for continuity, deeper technical sessions, structured mentorship, and stronger engagement of investors and financial institutions.

FAQs

Who should attend IMBS 2.0?

IMBS 2.0 is designed for Muslim entrepreneurs, professionals, startup founders, business owners, students, investors, and anyone interested in ethical, non-interest and halal-based enterprise.

Do I need to own a business before attending?

No. Whether you are already running a business, planning to start one, or simply interested in Islamic finance and ethical enterprise, IMBS 2.0 will provide valuable insights and opportunities for you.

What makes IMBS 2.0 different from other business events?

IMBS 2.0 is built on Islamic principles of fairness, trust, and social responsibility. It combines business strategy, faith-based ethics, and real-world enterprise solutions in a way that few business conferences offer.

Will there be networking opportunities?

Yes. IMBS 2.0 provides structured and informal networking sessions where participants can connect with Muslim business leaders, investors, professionals, and potential partners.

How do I register for IMBS 2.0?

Registration will be done online. A registration link will be shared through official IMBS platforms and social media channels once it opens. Seats are limited and early registration is strongly encouraged.
